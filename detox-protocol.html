DETOX PROTOCOL (DP)
Detox Protocol (DP) is a patented supplement designed to support the body’s natural detox pathways. It features a blend of targeted nutrients and herbal extracts that help bind and eliminate environmental toxins, heavy metals, and metabolic by-products while supporting liver, kidney, and gastrointestinal function.

Daily environmental exposures from air pollution, radiation, processed foods, and chemicals place a significant burden on our detox systems. DP provides highly bioavailable forms of sulfur, antioxidants, and phytonutrients to help maintain cellular health and promote efficient phase I and phase II liver detoxification. Its formula also includes compounds that support methylation and glutathione production—critical processes for neutralizing reactive molecules and facilitating excretion.

Unlike generic multivitamins, Detox Protocol focuses on optimizing the body’s ability to metabolize and clear toxins. Regular use may help reduce oxidative stress, improve energy levels, support cognitive clarity, and promote overall well‑being. DP is suitable for individuals looking to support healthy detoxification during periods of increased exposure or as part of an ongoing wellness regimen.

KEY BENEFITS:

  • 1) Liver Support: Supplies nutrients that support both phase I & phase II detoxification pathways, enhancing metabolism of toxins.
  • 2) Heavy Metal Binding: Contains chelating agents and sulfur-rich compounds to help bind and remove metals like mercury, lead, and cadmium.
  • 3) Antioxidant Protection: Provides glutathione precursors and other antioxidants to neutralize free radicals generated during detox.
  • 4) Methylation Support: Includes methyl donors essential for proper detox enzyme function and neurotransmitter balance.
  • 5) Cellular Health: Supports membrane integrity and energy production to help cells cope with toxic insults.
  • 6) Gastrointestinal Cleansing: Ingredients that promote healthy bowel movements and microbial balance to facilitate toxin elimination.

Detox Protocol (DP) is formulated to be gentle yet comprehensive, making it appropriate for daily use. It is non-GMO, gluten-free, and formulated without artificial additives.
