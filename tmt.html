TRAINING/MENTORING/TUTORING

We do offer a range of educational support to all health professionals depending on their needs e.g. returning to practice, tutoring to become a professional, mentoring to develop personal skills and goal achievement which would lead to personal and professional development (PPD).

Training, Mentoring & Tutoring

Led by Dr. John Osuku Opio — a qualified pharmacist with extensive experience in integrative medicine, clinical practice, and professional education — we deliver tailored educational support to health professionals at every career stage. Whether you're returning to practice, pursuing professional qualifications, building advanced skills, or achieving personal and professional development (PPD) goals, our programs provide expert guidance, practical knowledge, and structured support for lasting growth.

We customize our approach to your specific needs — from refresher tutoring and competency-building sessions to one-on-one mentoring focused on leadership, integrative therapeutics, goal setting, and career advancement — empowering you to excel in your field with confidence and competence.

Training, Mentoring & Tutoring Details

Our programs are flexible, needs-based, and designed exclusively for health professionals. We focus on real-world application, evidence-informed practice, and holistic growth — helping you bridge gaps, regain confidence, qualify professionally, or advance your career through structured, supportive learning.

  • Tailored tutoring for professional qualifications and competency exams
  • Support for returning to practice after a career break
  • One-on-one mentoring for personal skill development and leadership
  • Goal-setting and achievement strategies for PPD (Personal & Professional Development)
  • Integrative focus incorporating herbal, conventional, and evidence-based approaches
  • Confidential, flexible formats (in-person, virtual, or hybrid) to fit your schedule
