MAGNESIUM MODULATOR COMPLEX
Magnesium modulator complex is a cutting-edge blend based on today's exploring field of cellular health and the underlying fascinating research. It is composed of Magnesium Malate and Magnesium Bisglycinate which is the most absorbable salt forms and intermediate in Krebs cycle, Boron in the form of Sodium Borate, Spirulina (Arthrospira platensis) as a reinforcing source of natural Magnesium in combination with other trace elements as copper, iron, calcium, manganese, etc and vitamins K1, K2, fibre and protein, hence, an energy precursor for cell activity.

Magnesium is a co-factor that drives well over 800 different biochemical enzymatic processes in the body, that are directly linked to cell maintenance, many of which contribute to the production of energy and vascular function. Hence, a crucial trace mineral in cellular health once termed the king or the queen of minerals.

Magnesium as a macro-element is one of the most frequent and common nutritional, clinical and sub-clinical deficiency and insufficiency encountered. Statistics show that an estimated 40% of the western adult population have lower magnesium level. Recent estimates reveal that almost 80% of globe population is deficient in magnesium.

Studies have shown a direct link between magnesium deficiency and cancer, as well as increasing magnesium through magnesium therapy and reduced tumour growth. There could be a previously unknown link between magnesium deficiency and aging since a deficiency of magnesium in cultured human fibroblasts (connective tissue) accelerates the role at which cells turned into "zombie cells".

Magnesium works as a middleman to balance the activity of electrolytes like potassium and sodium in our cellular ecosystem and assists to transport those electrolytes through each cell's delicate outer cell. A 1984 study demonstrated magnesium's cellular electrolyte transfer mechanism to be critical for supporting coronary and heart health.

Holistic health pioneer Doctor Norman Shealy did mention that magnesium deficiency may be responsible for more diseases than any other nutrient. That is a very powerful statement.

  • 3) Circulation & Bone Health: Magnesium is needed for blood circulation, healthy teeth, bone formation, muscle coordination, energy since it stabilizes phosphate in ATP and then affects all tissues in the body due to ubiquitous energy (ATP) demands. Magnesium balances blood sugar levels, blood pressure levels and kidney function.
  • 4) Anti-Aging: Magnesium fights cellular aging and protects genetics involved in DNA synthesis, the process of preserving and rebuilding your body genetics.
  • 5) Cardiovascular Support: Magnesium is critical for supporting coronary and heart health, facilitates nerve and muscle function, regulates pain responses.
  • 6) Inflammation & Sleep: Magnesium helps regulation of inflammation, balances hormone and sleep cycles.

The benefit of magnesium is infinite due to its capacity in manning a range of complex biological processes which are the touchstone of human health, wellness and vitality.

BORON

  • 3. Inflammation Response: Promotes a healthy inflammation response by reducing levels of inflammatory biomarkers in the body e.g. hs-CRP connected with a wide range of serious health issues. For example, one study expressed reduction by 50%.
