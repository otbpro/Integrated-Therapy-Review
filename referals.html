REFERRALS

In order to diagnose any form of illness after a clinical assessment or consultation, we usually refer patients to undertake a range of laboratory tests or scans to investigate their health status. Sometimes they can also be referred to an appropriate practitioner to safeguard against time.

Referrals

Led by Dr. John Osuku Opio — a qualified pharmacist with deep expertise in integrative medicine and health science — our practice prioritizes accurate diagnosis and comprehensive care. Following a thorough clinical assessment or consultation, we refer patients to trusted diagnostic services or appropriate specialists as needed.

Referrals may include a range of laboratory tests, imaging scans, or consultations with other qualified practitioners. This ensures precise investigation of health status, timely intervention, and safeguards against delays in care — supporting optimal outcomes whether integrating with conventional treatments or pursuing holistic approaches.

Referral Services Details

Our referral process is patient-centered and evidence-based. After a detailed clinical assessment or consultation, we provide targeted referrals to ensure accurate diagnosis and appropriate management — preventing unnecessary delays and promoting holistic, integrative health outcomes.

  • Referrals for laboratory tests (blood work, pathology, etc.)
  • Referrals for diagnostic imaging scans (MRI, CT, ultrasound, etc.)
  • Referrals to appropriate specialists or practitioners
  • Timely safeguarding of health through precise investigations
  • Integration with conventional or alternative therapies as needed
  • Professional, documented referrals for seamless care coordination
